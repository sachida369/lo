Frequently asked questions

Is this service completely free?

Yes, our loan comparison and eligibility check service is completely free. You only pay processing fees to the lender once you accept an offer.

How soon will I be contacted?

We aim to respond within 24 hours via email. For faster response, use our WhatsApp chat feature for immediate assistance.

What documents do I need?

Basic documents include ID proof, address proof, income proof (salary slips/ITR), and bank statements. Specific requirements vary by loan type.

Do you charge any hidden fees?

No hidden fees from our side. All charges are transparent and communicated upfront. Lender fees are clearly mentioned in offers.

Can I apply for multiple loan types?

Yes, you can apply for different loan types. We'll help you understand which option best suits your financial needs.
